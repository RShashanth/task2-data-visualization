Task 2 – Data Visualization & Storytelling (Power BI)

Objective:
This project focuses on creating a data-driven story using Power BI dashboards by analyzing Superstore sales data.

Dataset Used:
. superstore_india_1000_region.csv
  (1000 rows with correct City–State–Region mapping)
. Tools Used
. Power BI Desktop
. Power Query
. DAX Measures

KPIs Implemented:
. Total Sales
. Total Profit
. Profit Margin %
. Total Orders
. Average Order Value

Visuals Included:
. KPI Cards
. Sales Trend Line Chart
. Sales by Category Bar Chart
. Filled Map (State-wise Sales)
. Top 10 Sub-Categories Table
. Region/Segment/Category/Year Slicers

Files Included in This Repository:
. task2_dashboard.pbix – Power BI dashboard file
. dashboard.pdf – Exported report
. superstore_india_1000_region.csv – Dataset
. Screenshots

Outcome:
This dashboard provides clear visual storytelling and business insights on sales performance, profitability, and regional trends.
